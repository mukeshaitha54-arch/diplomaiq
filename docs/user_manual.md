# User Manual

## 1. Getting Started
1. Navigate to the DiplomaIQ homepage.
2. Click **Get Started** to create an account.
3. Check your email for the OTP verification code.
4. Once verified, log in.

## 2. Syncing SBTET Records
Upon your first login, your dashboard will be empty.
1. Click **Sync Records** from the sidebar.
2. Enter your SBTET Hall Ticket Number.
3. The system will securely pull your Semester SGPA, backlogs, and attendance records.
4. Return to the **Command Center** to view your populated dashboard.

## 3. The Command Center
- **Action Center:** Your AI-prioritized task list (e.g., "Critical: Recover 2 backlogs").
- **Metrics:** Quick glances at your CGPA, Attendance, and overall Health Score.
- **Charts:** View your SGPA trend across semesters.

## 4. Academic Analytics & AI Coach
- Navigate to the **Coach** tab.
- Click **Generate Academic Report** to receive a comprehensive 11-point analysis of your performance.
- Use the **PDF Export** button to download the report for your records.
- Click **Generate Study Plan** for day-by-day exam prep guidance.

## 5. ECET Advisor
- Navigate to the **ECET Engine**.
- Select your target branch (e.g., CSE) and Category (e.g., EWS).
- Enter your expected or actual ECET Rank.
- Click **Find Colleges**. The system will present Dream, Moderate, and Safe colleges based on historical cutoffs, accompanied by an AI explanation.
